MISSION

Offer information and computing programs that provide concrete theoretical, ethical, spiritual and experimental grounds for research, internships and multi- disciplinary programs; Conduct research for development and societal progress; Foster graduates to possess leadership skills and social consciousness; Provide professional expertise in partnerships with industries, educational institutions and organizations to improve quality of life.
